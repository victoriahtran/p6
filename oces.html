For my research project, I am continuing the development of Cerebro, a location-based mobile/web photo-sharing and chat app, built with Meteor.js, Blaze, and React. The aim of our app is to connect two strangers through the idea of shared experience, using pictures of everyday moments in life.

I am working with a partner, Cindy Hu, a senior at Northwestern University. We are jointly being mentored by Ryan Louie, a current PhD candidate at Northwestern University. We have received an undergraduate research grant worth $2,000 to fund our user study.
